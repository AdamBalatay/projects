Audi TT RS

2022 was the last model year for the TT RS in the U.S. With an even more dynamic look for the top of the TT model line, the TT RS featured a 2.5-liter engine delivering a distinctive five-cylinder sound, which harkened back to the original turbocharged five-cylinder of the Ur-quattro. The TT RS celebrated its 10-year anniversary from its introduction as a 2012 model year in the U.S. market.
